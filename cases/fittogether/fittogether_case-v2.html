02 — Who it's for02 — 为谁设计

Two users anchored every later decision.两个用户，撑住了后面每一个决策。

A busy professional and an active parent. They kept the design honest about whose friction I was actually solving — so features earned their place against a person, not a hunch. 一个忙碌的上班族，一个要带娃的家长。他们让设计始终对得上“到底在解谁的麻烦” —— 每个功能都得对着一个具体的人站住脚，而不是凭感觉。

Concept personas · working assumptions, not field research概念用户假设 · 非真实用研

“I love staying active, but it's hard to stick to a routine on my own. I miss the social aspect of working out with others and need a way to stay connected and motivated.”“我喜欢保持活力，但一个人很难坚持。我想念和别人一起运动的那种社交感，需要一个方式让自己有连接、有动力。”

Brief story简述

Jessica is a 29-year-old graphic designer at a marketing agency, living alone in a small city apartment. She has always been moderately active — yoga, the occasional run — but a sedentary job and busy schedule make a consistent routine hard. Working remotely, she feels isolated and is looking for ways to stay fit and stay connected.Jessica，29 岁，在一家营销公司做平面设计，独居在小城市的小公寓。她一直算比较活跃——瑜伽、偶尔跑步——但久坐的工作和忙碌的节奏让她很难维持规律运动。远程办公让她有些孤立，她在找既能保持身材、又能建立连接的方式。

Goals目标
  • Maintain fitness:保持运动： build a consistent routine for health and energy.建立规律，改善身体和精力。
  • Social connection:社交连接： meet new people and build a network.认识新朋友，建立社交圈。
  • Mental well-being:心理健康： reduce stress through regular activity.通过规律运动缓解压力。
  • Stay motivated:保持动力： find external accountability and support.寻找外部督促和支持。
Frustrations痛点
  • Time management:时间管理： work crowds out regular exercise.工作挤占了规律运动的时间。
  • Social isolation:社交孤立： remote work cut her social interaction.远程办公减少了社交机会。
  • Lack of motivation:缺乏动力： hard to stay consistent alone.一个人很难坚持。
  • Limited space:空间有限： a small apartment limits what she can do at home.小公寓限制了在家能做的运动。

“I want to stay healthy and active, but it's hard to find the time with work and family. It would be great to find a way to combine fitness with spending time with my kids.”“我想保持健康和活力，但工作和家庭让我很难抽出时间。要是能把健身和陪孩子的时间结合起来就好了。”

Brief story简述

Mark is a 45-year-old software engineer living in the suburbs with his wife and two kids, aged 8 and 12. He has always been fairly active, but a demanding job and family responsibilities make a consistent routine difficult. He values quality time with his family and wants to fit fitness into that busy life rather than around it.Mark，45 岁，软件工程师，和妻子、两个孩子（8 岁和 12 岁）住在郊区。他一直比较活跃，但繁重的工作和家庭责任让规律运动变得困难。他看重和家人相处的时间，想把健身融进忙碌的生活，而不是挤在生活之外。

Goals目标
  • Stay fit and healthy:保持健康： keep up the energy to match his kids.有精力跟上孩子。
  • Family involvement:家庭参与： work out with family, set a healthy example.和家人一起运动，树立健康榜样。
  • Stress relief:释放压力： use fitness to unwind from work.用运动从工作中解压。
  • Social interaction:社交互动： connect with other dads and families.和其他父母、家庭建立连接。
Frustrations痛点
  • Time constraints:时间紧张： work and family leave little room to exercise.工作和家庭几乎不留运动余地。
  • Family commitments:家庭责任： responsibilities limit personal fitness time.限制了个人健身时间。
  • Motivation:动力： hard to stay driven without a routine or community.没有规律或社群很难坚持。
  • Family-friendly options:亲子友好： needs activities that work for adults and kids.需要适合大人和孩子一起的活动。

08 — Summary08 — 总结

What this case actually proves.这个案例真正能证明的。

Four conclusions, one through-line — reason from the user, not from a pile of features. 四条结论，一条主线——从用户出发推导，而不是从功能堆叠。

User Insights用户洞察

A flexible information hierarchy accommodates diverse users, from beginners to busy professionals.健身用户群体差异很大（初学者、健身达人、上班族、宝妈等），信息架构和引导流程需要保持灵活。

Long-term consistency is fueled by goal tracking and social accountability, not just exercise instructions.用户的持续动力往往来自目标追踪和社交支持，设计不能只强调锻炼动作本身。

Experience Flow体验流程

Users are usually “on the go,” so the flow needs minimal steps to reach their primary activity fast.用户大多处于“运动中”或“准备运动”的状态，交互要尽量减少步骤、快速直达。

Background features like coaching cues and data recording have to keep running to prevent app fatigue.音乐播放、数据记录、AI 教练提示等功能要考虑后台运行，避免用户频繁切换应用。

Monetization & Strategy功能与商业化结合

The challenge is surfacing premium features — analysis, AI coaching, challenges — without disrupting the core experience.如何在不打扰用户的前提下，展示订阅或付费功能（高级分析、AI 教练、社区挑战）是一大挑战。

A tiered approach offers foundational data for free, then gradually reveals advanced premium value.用渐进式解锁引导转化：先提供免费基础数据，再逐步展示更详细的高级功能。

Reflections设计反思

Success is the appeal of the “30th workout” — long-term habit formation over a strong first impression.健身高频但容易流失，设计不仅要关注“第一次使用”，更要关注“第 30 次使用”是否依然有吸引力。

High-value design prioritizes findability and simplicity over a feature-heavy, complex interface.用户最常抱怨的不是功能不够多，而是“太复杂 / 找不到入口”——简洁和清晰始终是第一优先级。
